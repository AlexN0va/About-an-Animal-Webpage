Habitat Details

The shoebill is often found in swamps and marshed that are dense, extensive and have what they are looking for, like lungfish, papyrus, deep water, and poorly oxygenated water. Their distrubution mainly depednds on their diet in Central Africa. Habitat destruction, spoecifically is a cotributor to their decline in numbers.
